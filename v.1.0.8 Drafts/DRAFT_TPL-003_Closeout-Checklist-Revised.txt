version aligns with the field-tested 5-step closeout protocol (Section

TPL-003: SESSION CLOSEOUT CHECKLIST

Template ID: GOV-AIPM-TPL-003
Version: 2.0
Purpose: Ensure consistent session endings with complete documentation,
         proper handoff preparation, and no lost work or context.
When to Use: At the end of every AI chat session, triggered by the user
             saying "close out" or equivalent.

Cross-reference: Section 4.3.3 (Session Closeout), TPL-005 (Session
Log), TPL-002 (Bootstrap Template)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INSTRUCTIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This checklist structures the closeout sequence defined in Section 4.3.3.
The AI initiates this checklist ONLY when the user triggers closeout.
The AI never auto-initiates closeout.

The checklist is divided into two parts:
- Part A: AI Responsibilities (Steps 1-5 of the closeout protocol)
- Part B: User Responsibilities (post-session actions)

The AI works through Part A during the session. Part B is performed by
the user after the session ends.

Tip: Request this checklist from the AI near the end of a session:
"Close out" or "Let's close out." The AI will work through the
protocol, beginning with the loose ends assessment.

DIALOG COMPLETION OPTION:
The AI executes this checklist as part of the closeout sequence. No
special prompt is needed beyond the closeout trigger.

TEMPLATE NOTES
================================================================================

COMPARISON WITH v1.0 (PREVIOUS VERSION):

The v1.0 template had 10 flat sections mixing AI and user actions. The
v2.0 template restructures around:
- The 5-step closeout protocol from Section 4.3.3
- Clear Part A (AI) / Part B (User) separation
- Loose ends assessment as the entry point (not a deliverables checklist)

Sections from v1.0 that are consolidated or relocated:
- "Work Completed This Session" → now captured in Session Log, not the
  checklist
- "Decisions Made" → assessed in Step 1 (Loose Ends), documented in
  Session Log
- "Files Generated" → assessed in Step 1 (Artifacts), listed in
  Session Log
- "Chat Transcript Export" → moved to Part B (User Responsibilities)
- "Bootstrap Status" → now Step 5 artifact generation
- "File Management" → moved to Part B (User Responsibilities)
- "Session Documentation" → now Step 5 (the checklist doesn't document
  what the Session Log already documents)
- "Next Session Preparation" → now captured in Bootstrap
- "Quality Check" → replaced by the SFR in the Session Log
- "Final Confirmation" → simplified to "User confirmed: proceed"

The revised checklist is shorter and more action-oriented. The Session
Log (TPL-005) now carries the documentation burden; the checklist
focuses on ensuring the process executes correctly.
