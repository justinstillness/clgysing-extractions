**TPL-003: SESSION CLOSEOUT CHECKLIST**\
\
**Template ID:** GOV-AIPM-TPL-003\
**Version:** 2.0\
**Purpose:** Ensure consistent session endings with complete documentation, proper handoff preparation, and no lost work or context.\
**When to Use:** At the end of every AI chat session, triggered by the user saying \"close out\" or equivalent.\
**Cross-reference:** Section 4.3.3 (Session Closeout), TPL-005 (Session Log), TPL-002 (Bootstrap Template)\
\
---\
\
### **INSTRUCTIONS**\
\
This checklist structures the closeout sequence defined in Section 4.3.3. The AI initiates this checklist **ONLY** when the user triggers closeout. The AI never auto-initiates closeout.\
\
The checklist is divided into two parts:\
*   **Part A: AI Responsibilities** (Steps 1...5 of the closeout protocol)\
*   **Part B: User Responsibilities** (post-session actions)\
\
The AI works through Part A during the session. Part B is performed by the user after the session ends.\
\
**Tip:** Request this checklist from the AI near the end of a session: *\"Close out\"* or *\"Let's close out.\"* The AI will work through the protocol, beginning with the loose ends assessment.\
