### **4. Impact on Result Quality**\
The research established a distinction between \"Threaded\" and \"Unthreaded\" query strategies for different use cases:\
*   **Threaded (Chained):** Best for **depth accumulation** and **coverage expansion**. It allows the model to refine definitions over time and paginate through larger sets of information, though it requires post-hoc deduplication due to the ~30% overlap [7], [6].\
*   **Unthreaded (Independent):** Best for **consistency checks**. Running identical queries without threading acts as fresh sampling, which can verify if a concept is consistently identified as a priority by the model [7].\
